What we do


Shelter

There are 1.77 million homeless people in India, or 0.15% of the country's total population, according to the 2011 census.
We provide the poor people the shelter for survival so that they don't sleep on the road


Food

Around 5,275,603 people died from hunger over the world,this year (2020-2021)
We supply good quality of groceries once in a month to them for as much as family we can so that they dont die due to hunger .
